What All Three Agents Agree On

Seven bedrock facts established through CRITIC-FACTUAL review. These form the fixed substrate beneath all projections and strategic recommendations.

GLP-1 Costs Are the Dominant MLR Numerator Input
2024 global revenue exceeds $50B. U.S. WAC list prices: ~$936–$1,350/month. Net-after-rebate cost estimated at $450–$750/month per treated member — the largest single drug category expense for most commercial payers.
High · Primary — Manufacturer Earnings
No Generic/Biosimilar Relief Before 2031–2032
Semaglutide compound patent '343 expires December 2031; settlement terms point to 2032 generic entry. Only available cost-reduction levers by 2026: brand-on-brand rebate competition, UM, and the BALANCE model.
High · Primary — Patent Records
SELECT Trial CVD Label: Fully Verified
20% MACE reduction (HR 0.80, 95% CI 0.72–0.90, NNT≈67 over ~34 months). FDA CVD risk reduction label for Wegovy: March 8, 2024. This is the single most important clinical evidence driving coverage expansion beyond diabetes.
High · Primary — FDA Label / NEJM
Prior Authorization: Near-Universal Baseline
PA for obesity-indication GLP-1s now covers ~100% of prescriptions, up from 3–5% in 2020. This is the universal baseline UM tool across all payer types — but the effectiveness depends on denial rates that are not publicly disclosed.
High · Primary — Definitive HC Analysis
Adherence: 32–63% at 12 Months (Corrected Range)
Wide range reflects genuine heterogeneity: 2021 cohorts on older agents showed ~32% persistence; 2024 cohorts on Wegovy/Zepbound show ~63%. Adherence is the most critical modifier of both cost projections and offset calculations.
High · Secondary — Real-World Persistence Studies
2026 Is Too Short for Most Offset Categories
Costs accrue immediately; offsets require 2–8+ years sustained treatment. The 2026 horizon captures near-zero offset for most commercial populations except HF+obesity (6–18 month offset) and bariatric displacement (1–3 year).
High · Convergence — All 3 Agents
Prescription Volumes: Exponential, Obesity Fastest
Directional trajectory is uncontested across all agents. Precise aggregate figures lack verified IQVIA primary sourcing — the specific claim of 59M→100M+ Rx (2022→2023) should be treated with caution. Monthly volumes and obesity-specific >200% YoY growth are directionally supported.†
