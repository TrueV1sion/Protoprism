Policy Landscape 03 / 15

The 10 Policy Vectors Reshaping U.S. Healthcare

The SOTU crystallized a unified healthcare restructuring that simultaneously disrupts every major payment and delivery mechanism.

1. The Great Healthcare Plan

Direct payments to individuals via HSAs. Eliminate PBM kickbacks. Codify MFN drug pricing. "Plain English" insurance standard. Announced Jan 15, 2026.

SEVERE | Restructures insurance economics

2. One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA)

$1.02T Medicaid cuts (CBO). Work requirements 80 hrs/mo starting Dec 2026. Payment caps 100-110% Medicare. 29+ states see cuts.

SEVERE | Largest Medicaid restructuring since 1965

3. MA Rate Shock

2027 proposed: 0.09% (vs. 5.1% in 2026). Unlinked CRR exclusion from risk scoring: -1.53%. Humana: $3.5B profit headwind. UNH -20.7%, HUM -22%.

SEVERE | End of MA "gold rush"

4. ACA Subsidy Expiration

Premiums +114% to $1,904/yr avg. 4.8M more uninsured (Urban Inst.). $32.1B lost provider revenue. 340K jobs at risk.

HIGH | Largest coverage loss since ACA

5. PBM Reform (H.R. 7148)

Delinking effective Jan 1, 2028. ERISA rebate pass-through Jan 1, 2029. FTC enforcement: Express Scripts settled; CVS/Optum suits continue.

MODERATE | 18-month implementation window

6. MFN Drug Pricing / TrumpRx

16 pharma deals; 43 drugs on TrumpRx.gov. PhRMA blocking MFN codification. Those 16 companies raised prices on 872 other drugs (+4% median, Jan 2026).

MIXED | Limited scope, industry gamesmanship

7. GLP-1 Coverage Expansion

Medicare demo July 2026; BALANCE model Jan 2027. $245-350/mo MFN price; $50 copay cap. Part D spending: $27.5B gross (2024). 13 states covering via Medicaid.

POSITIVE | Major benefit; massive fiscal exposure

8. V28 Risk Adjustment

Full phase-in 2026. Upcoding crackdown. Combined with unlinked CRR exclusion = significant effective rate reduction. Note: CMS is NOT eliminating chart reviews — only excluding diagnoses from chart review records not linked to qualifying encounters.

HIGH | Coding recalibration required

9. Price Transparency

New hospital pricing rules Jan 2026 (enforcement delayed to Apr 2026). "Plain English" insurance standard. Machine-readable file requirements expanded.

MODERATE | $2-5B compliance cost

10. Vaccine Schedule Reduction

17 → 11 childhood vaccines. Pre-vaccine: rotavirus = 200K+ ER visits/yr. Secretary Kennedy driving chronic disease agenda at HHS.

HIGH RISK | Public health consequences

Beyond Healthcare: SOTU Forces That Reshape the Market

The 107-minute SOTU covered economy, tariffs, AI, energy, immigration, and foreign policy — each with transmission mechanisms into healthcare.

AI & Energy: Rate Payer Protection Pledge

Trump announced tech companies must build their own power plants for AI data centers. Data center energy demand expected to triple by 2028.

Healthcare IT impact: Cloud-hosted analytics platforms face infrastructure cost uncertainty. On-site generation could lower costs for health systems with co-located data centers.

MODERATE | Health IT infrastructure cost uncertainty

Tariffs & Supreme Court

SCOTUS struck down Trump's global tariff policy. Trump called ruling "very unfortunate." Tariff uncertainty persists.

Healthcare impact: Medical device import costs volatile. Pharmaceutical API supply chains (60%+ from India/China) face potential disruption. Hospital CapEx budgeting frozen.

MODERATE | Supply chain & device cost volatility

Economy & Affordability

Core CPI at 2.5% (lowest since Apr 2021). GDP growth 2.2%. Dow crossed 50K, S&P hit 7K.

Healthcare impact: Consumer out-of-pocket pressure remains elevated. Employer-sponsored coverage cost trend (6-7%) outpacing wage growth.

NEUTRAL | Consumer pressure continues

Immigration & Workforce

Border enforcement emphasized; immigration down 80%+. 50 Democratic lawmakers boycotted.

Healthcare impact: 26% of physicians, 16% of nurses are immigrants — face visa delays. Nursing shortage worsens in rural areas coinciding with 300+ hospital closures.

MODERATE | Workforce shortage accelerates

Iran Military Buildup

Largest U.S. military buildup in Middle East since 2003. Two carrier groups + 14 warships deployed.

Healthcare impact: VA/TRICARE budget exposure if engagement escalates. Military medical reserve activation would strain civilian healthcare workforce.

LOW-MOD | Contingent on escalation

Housing & Retirement

Institutional investor home-buying ban proposed. Government-backed 401(k) for workers without employer match.

Healthcare impact: Retirement savings expansion could reduce future Medicare pressure. Housing stability correlates with health outcomes.

LOW-MOD | Indirect positive

Emergent Insights: 8-Agent Convergence

Patterns no single agent identified independently — arising from the intersection of all eight analytical dimensions.

1. The Medicaid Domino Effect

$1.02T cascades through MCO revenue → 29-state cuts → 300+ closures → uninsured surge → +$40-60B uncompensated care → consolidation wave → health IT demand. 8/8 agents converged.

2. The Paradox of Compression

Margin compression increases technology spend. Flat MA rates make Star Ratings, risk adjustment, and fraud recovery the only margin levers. The worse it gets for payers, the better for their tech vendors.

3. The Regulatory Complexity Flywheel

V28 → coding recalibration → audit data → enforcement → more analytics demand. PBM reform → rebate tracking → transparency → oversight. Interoperability APIs → standardization → further leverage. Self-reinforcing TAM expansion.

4. The Two-Speed Healthcare System

Commercially insured/affluent benefit (PBM reform, GLP-1, HSAs). Medicaid-dependent/rural/low-income face coverage loss, 300+ closures. 30% Black / 25% Hispanic on Medicaid = disproportionate impact.

5. The GLP-1 Fiscal Time Bomb

Part D GLP-1: $27.5B gross (2024) — 5x from 2019 — BEFORE obesity coverage. Full uptake could exceed $100B/yr. May consume more Medicare budget than OBBBA saves.

6. The Second Reconciliation Amplifier NEW

Legislative Pipeline Agent reveals a second reconciliation bill is likely (Q3-Q4 2026). RSC pushing deeper FMAP cuts, site-neutral ($146B). OBBBA effects here are the floor, not the ceiling.
