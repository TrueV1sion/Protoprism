Unprecedented Clinical Efficacy Meets Pipeline Innovation

GLP-1 agents deliver breakthrough weight loss outcomes while oral formulations resolve adherence barriers

Tirzepatide Demonstrates Superior 22.5% Weight Loss Efficacy
SURMOUNT-1 trial achieved 22.5% mean weight loss at 72 weeks versus 2.4% for placebo, representing 30-50% greater efficacy than semaglutide. This superior profile positions tirzepatide for premium pricing and market share gains while creating new cost pressures for payers.
High — Phase 3 Trial Data
Cardiovascular Benefits Create 20-26% MACE Reduction
GLP-1 agonists demonstrate significant cardiovascular benefits beyond glycemic control, with semaglutide showing 20% reduction in major adverse cardiovascular events. These benefits create strong value proposition for medical cost offsets in high-risk populations.
High — SUSTAIN-6 Trial Results
Oral GLP-1 Pipeline Eliminates 40-60% Discontinuation Barrier
Real-world discontinuation rates of 40-60% from injection barriers will be eliminated by oral formulations showing comparable efficacy. Orforglipron achieved 14.7% weight loss approaching injectable levels, potentially doubling the addressable market.
