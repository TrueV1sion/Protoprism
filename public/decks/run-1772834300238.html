Pipeline Cartographer Drug Landscape & Pipeline Economics

The 2026 GLP-1 Market:
Duopoly, Patents & Pipeline Events

No biosimilar competition before ~2032. Peak-WAC duopoly dominates. One near-term entrant changes everything — but not in the way payers expect.

Dominant Commercial Agents (2026)
Semaglutide (Ozempic/Wegovy/Rybelsus) and tirzepatide (Mounjaro/Zepbound) anchor the market. Combined 2024 U.S. revenues ~$40B (corrected from agents' $50B+ overestimate). WAC: $969–$1,349/month for weekly injectables. No biosimilar entry before ~2032 — semaglutide core patent expires December 5, 2031.
High — Primary Sources (Novo, Lilly Earnings)
Orforglipron: FDA Action Q2 2026
Eli Lilly oral non-peptide GLP-1. NDA filed late 2025, FDA action expected Q2 2026 for obesity. Oral formulation eliminates cold-chain; lower manufacturing cost enables 20–40% formulary price concessions. The most significant competitive event of the 2026 window — but see Slide 10 for why this likely worsens MLR despite lower per-unit cost.
High — Primary (Lilly Investor Site, BioPharma Dive Dec 2025)
FDA Indication Expansions (2024–2025)
CV risk reduction (Wegovy, Mar 2024) · OSA (Zepbound, Dec 20, 2024) · CKD (Ozempic, Jan 2025) · MASH semaglutide decision anticipated mid-2025. Each creates new legally incontestable coverage obligations expanding the addressable population and shrinking PA denial surface.
